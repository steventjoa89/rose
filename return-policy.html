Return Policy


REFUNDS

Our refund policy lasts 14 days. If 14 days have gone by since the delivery date, unfortunately, we can’t offer you a refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused (brand new) and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

RETURN

1. Please be sure to save your postal receipt. Ship only with your country's Postal Service.
2. You will be responsible for shipping costs.
3. We highly recommend you insure your parcel and adequately protect it during shipment, and it's original packing. ROSÉ is not responsible for products lost or damaged during shipment.

Please send the jewelry unused/unworn back to ROSÉ with a completed Return Form, original box, and the original warranty card.

No jewelry will be accepted without the return form. Must Contact us at info@rose.com to learn how to return your product.

CAREFUL: In order to receive a refund or exchange on items returned, the jewelry has to be returned (i) if you notified us within 14 days from the delivery date; (ii) with completed return form; (iii) in the original packaging; (iv) in new/unused condition. We do not accept jewelry with any worn signs.

1. If you are unable to comply fully with the return policy, unfortunately, you may not be entitled to a full refund or replacement.
2. Price adjustments on previous purchases are not permitted.
3. Returns can only be accepted on purchases from ROSÉ Co. at www.rose.com

Some returns will be subject to restocking fees such as:

1. initial delivery cost if the jewelry is returned not because there is a fault on ROSÉ's side.
2. if the jewelry returned has been worn, dirt on the item, scratches on the item etc.
