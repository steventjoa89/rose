6. Cookies

If you have set your browser to accept cookies, we will use them on different pages. These are text files that are stored on your device and allow the exchange of data with our system. This exchange facilitates the use of our website, as certain information need not be repeated constantly.

Session cookies: These are deleted after the end of the browser session.

Long-term cookies: These remain on your device so that your browser can be recognized at the next session. This eliminates the need to re-enter the password or fill out forms with your customer data.

You can make a setting on your browser to be informed about the setting of cookies and, if necessary, to decide on their acceptance individually. The functionality of the website may be restricted if cookies are not accepted.
